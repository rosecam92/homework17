My UI/UX Journey

My origin story goes all the way back to an aspiring pre-med with a penchant for understanding the dynamic of human cognitive processes and behavior. Fast-forward to the trials and tribulations one usually encounters on such a path, I learned to accept my need for creative expression in harmony with continuous discovery and principles; hence my journey in the field of UI/UX design. As a designer, I see the product adapting to the user and not the other way around. In order to deliver such products, it is important to understand the user at their core and what they want to accomplish with, of course, a dash of visually appealing and responsive artifacts to elevate the user experience.
